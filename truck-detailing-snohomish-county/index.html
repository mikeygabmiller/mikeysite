What Makes Truck Detailing Different

The bed

This is where most shops half-ass it. I sweep, blow out, pressure wash, and treat bed liners. Spray-in liners get a UV protectant so they don't fade. Drop-ins come out and get cleaned underneath where mold loves to hide.

Wheel wells and underbody

Trucks build up serious mud and salt in the wheel wells. Pressure cleaning here prevents long-term rust on suspension components and frame.

Interior wear

Truck interiors take more punishment — work boots on carpets, tools in the cab, lunch on the dash. I shampoo carpets with a hot water extractor, treat leather with conditioner, and detail every storage compartment.

Lifted truck access

Lifted trucks are harder to wash without proper tools. I bring extending wash mitts and the right ladders to actually reach the roof and middle of the bed without leaving streaks.

Real Talk on Work Trucks If your truck is your office, I get it. I'll work around your schedule — early morning before you head out, evenings, weekends. The Work Truck Reset package is built for people who can't take a day off but need their rig cleaned.

Common Questions

Can you handle a really nasty work truck?

Yes. The Work Truck Reset is built for it. I've cleaned trucks with months of construction debris, dog hair compounded into seats, and mud caked into every wheel well. Nothing surprises me anymore.

Do you bring water and power?

Power, yes — gas generator. Water, sometimes — I usually use your spigot but I have a tank for properties without water access. Just let me know when booking.

How long does a full truck detail take?

3-5 hours for a full detail. Work Truck Reset can run 5-7 hours on really rough trucks. Basic refresh is closer to 2 hours.

Will you detail my truck if it's lifted/oversized?

Yes. Bigger trucks take longer and cost a bit more, but I have the gear to do it right.

Do you do recurring detailing for work trucks?

Yes — Clean Club has monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly tiers built for daily-use vehicles. Saves you money vs one-off bookings.
