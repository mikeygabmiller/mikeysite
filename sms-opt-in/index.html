SMS Opt-In & Consent

This page shows exactly how customers consent to receive text messages from Mikey's Mobile Detailing, and the full terms of our SMS program, for transparency and for carrier (A2P 10DLC) review.

How Customers Opt In

Customers opt in to SMS messages on our public website at mikeysdetailing.com. On the "Get a Quote" / Quick Quote form, after entering their name and phone number, the customer can check an optional SMS consent checkbox that sits directly above the "Lock in my quote" submit button.

The checkbox is unchecked by default, and consent is not required to receive a quote or any service, the form submits either way. Below is an exact reproduction of what the customer sees:

Next to the checkbox, customers can read our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions before opting in.

SMS Program Details

Program Name Mikey's Mobile Detailing SMS
Program Description Transactional, service-related text messages, quote confirmations, appointment scheduling, reminders, and follow-ups, sent only to customers who opt in via the checkbox on our quote form. We do not send marketing blasts.
Message Frequency Varies based on your service needs and appointment status.
Cost Msg & data rates may apply.
Opt Out Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You'll get one confirmation and no further texts.
Help Reply HELP, or contact us at book@mikeysdetailing.com or (425) 600-7897.
Privacy Your phone number and SMS consent are never shared with or sold to third parties for marketing. See our Privacy Policy.
