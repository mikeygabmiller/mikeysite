Stuff drivers ask before booking a polish.

Do you do paint correction in Lake Stevens, WA?

Yes. Lake Stevens is one of my core service cities. I cover Frontier Village, Hartford, Machias, Cavalero Hill, and the surrounding 98258 area. No travel surcharge.

How much does paint correction cost?

A basic Polish starts at $300. A 1-Step correction runs $450+. A full 2-Step correction is $700+. Final price depends on vehicle size and paint condition — use the instant quote tool for an exact number.

How long do the results last?

Paint correction permanently removes the scratches from your clear coat. As long as you use safe washing methods moving forward, the swirls will not come back.

How long does the job take?

A full correction takes anywhere from 5 to 10 hours depending on the tier. I do the entire job right in your driveway.

Paint correction vs regular wax — what's the difference?

Wax just temporarily hides light scratches for a few weeks. Paint correction physically levels the clear coat using machine polishers to permanently remove the defects.
