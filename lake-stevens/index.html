Why Lake Stevens

Detailing That Comes to Your Driveway

Lake Stevens drivers put their cars through a lot. Pollen off the trees in spring, road grime from SR-9 and SR-204, dust from the long drives between Frontier Village and downtown, and then there's the lake itself — sand from North Cove Park, dog hair from runs around Lundeen, the occasional drip from a wet wakeboard tossed in the back. By July your seats look more like a beach blanket than a car interior.

That's where I come in. I'm Mikey, and I run a one-man mobile detailing operation right here in Snohomish County. I'll pull into your driveway with a self-contained setup, spend a few hours bringing your car back to factory-fresh, and you don't pay me until you walk around it and tell me you love it. That's the deal — every time.

I've detailed over 300 cars across Snohomish County in the last 5 years — daily drivers, lifted trucks heading to the trails, family SUVs that haven't been deep-cleaned since the kids were toddlers, and yes, plenty of boats prepping for a weekend on the lake. If your car spends time in Lake Stevens, I know what it's been through, and I know how to fix it.

Common Questions

Lake Stevens FAQ

Stuff drivers ask before booking.

Do you come to my driveway in Lake Stevens?

Yes. I service all of Lake Stevens — Frontier Village, Cavalero Hill, downtown, the lake shore, Hartford. I just need a power outlet and a water spigot.

How much does mobile detailing cost?

Interior starts at $160. Exterior starts at $130. Full detail (interior + exterior) starts at $260. Final price depends on vehicle size and condition. Use the instant quote tool for an exact number.

How long does a full detail take?

First-time full details usually run 3–4 hours. If you're on the Clean Club recurring plan, maintenance details take about an hour because the car never gets that bad in the first place.

Do I need to be home?

No. Most Lake Stevens clients leave the keys, head to work or run errands, and come back to a finished car. I send before/after photos when done.

What if it rains?

I have a pop-up canopy that covers most cars under 6 feet tall. For inclement weather or larger vehicles, we can reschedule or you can drop off if you're nearby.

Do you do boats?

Yes — Lake Stevens has plenty of them. Boat detailing pricing is custom based on size and condition. Reach out for a quote.
