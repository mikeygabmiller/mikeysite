    Common Questions

    Everything You're Wondering

    Do you really come to me?

    Yep, that's the whole idea. I pull up to your home or work in Snohomish County with everything I need. You hand me the keys and get on with your day.

    Do I need to provide water or power?

    Yes. I'll need access to an outdoor water spigot and a power outlet. As long as those are reachable, I can detail even if you're not home, just point me to them when you book.

    How long does it take?

    A basic interior runs about 90 minutes, a full detail takes 3-5 hours depending on the size and condition of your car. I'll give you a clear time window when we confirm.

    What does it cost?

    Exterior details start at $160, interiors at $200, and a full inside-and-out detail starts at $299. Run the Quick Quote above for a price in about 30 seconds, no surprises, no hidden fees.

    When and how do I pay?

    You pay when I'm done and you're happy with it, not before. I take cash, card, and the usual apps.

    What if it rains?

    This is the PNW, so I work around the weather all the time. If it's pouring I'll reach out to reschedule. A lot of the work is interior or done under cover anyway.

    What if I'm not happy with it?

    We walk around the car together when I'm done. See something off? I fix it on the spot. Notice it later? I come back free. Still not happy? You get a full refund. Simple as that.
