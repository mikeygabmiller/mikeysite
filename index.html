Frequently Asked Questions

Click any question to see the answer

How long does a detail take?
Most full details take 2-4 hours depending on size and condition. Paint correction can take 6-8 hours or more.
Do you really come to my location?
Yes! I bring everything to your home or work in Snohomish, Monroe, Lake Stevens, Everett, and surrounding areas.
What forms of payment do you accept?
Cash, credit/debit cards, Venmo, Zelle, and Cash App. Payment is due when the job is complete.
How often should I get my car detailed?
Full detail every 4-6 months is ideal. Quick washes every 2-3 weeks keep things manageable between full details.
Do you offer monthly plans?
YES! I'm happy to schedule regular appointments at a discount for repeat customers — check out the Clean Club above.
What does a "full detail" include?
Both interior and exterior: vacuuming, shampooing, leather conditioning, hand wash, clay bar, wax, tire dressing, and window cleaning.
Can I get just interior or just exterior?
Absolutely. I offer interior-only and exterior-only packages.
Can you remove pet hair?
Yes, I have tools specifically for stubborn pet hair. Excessive amounts may add $20-40 to the price.
Do you use eco-friendly products?
I use high-quality, biodegradable products whenever possible that are safe for your car and the environment.
Can you fix scratches and swirl marks?
Yes, through paint correction. Light scratches and swirls can be removed. Deep scratches that go through clear coat need touch-up paint.
Do you clean wheels and tires?
Every exterior detail includes deep wheel and tire cleaning, plus tire dressing for that fresh look.
Can you remove smoke/pet/food odors?
Yes, I offer odor removal ($75-150) using deep cleaning and sanitization that actually eliminates odors instead of masking them.
Is waxing included?
Yes, all exterior and full detail packages include protective wax or sealant application.
What is a ceramic coating?
A liquid polymer that bonds to your paint, creating long-lasting protection (2-5 years) that's hydrophobic and enhances gloss.
How long does ceramic coating last?
Depending on the product and maintenance, 2-5+ years. Way longer than traditional wax (2-3 months).
What is a "clay bar" treatment?
Removes contaminants embedded in the paint (tree sap, tar, industrial fallout) that washing can't remove. Makes paint feel glass-smooth.
Do I need to empty my car first?
Yes, please remove all personal items from the interior, trunk, and glove box so I can clean thoroughly.
What if it rains on my appointment day?
If I can't work under a garage or covered area, we'll reschedule for free. I'm not doing half-assed work in the rain.
Does detailing increase resale value?
Absolutely. A clean, well-maintained car looks newer and can increase resale price significantly.
Do you clean engine bays?
Yes, engine bay cleaning is available as an add-on ($75-120). I safely degrease and dress the engine compartment.
Can you remove coffee or ink stains?
I use professional spot removers and have high success rates. Old or set-in stains might not come out 100%, but I'll be honest about it.
Can you fix foggy headlights?
Yes, headlight restoration ($80-120) removes oxidation and restores about 80% clarity on most headlights, then seals them with UV protectant.
What's the difference between polishing and waxing?
Polishing is abrasive — it removes imperfections. Waxing is protective — it adds a layer of shine and protection on top.
Do you guarantee your work?
100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with any part of the service, I'll make it right or refund you.
