FAQ

How long does a mobile car detail take in Snohomish County?

Most full details take 2–4 hours depending on size and condition. Bigger SUVs and trucks can take longer. I'll give you a realistic time estimate when you book.

Do you really come to my home or work location?

Yes. I bring everything — water, power, products, towels — to your home or work in Snohomish, Monroe, Lake Stevens, Everett, Bothell, Mill Creek, and Duvall.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash, credit/debit, Venmo, Zelle, and Cash App. Payment is due when the job is done and you've approved the work.

What is the Clean Club recurring detail membership?

Clean Club is a recurring detailing membership at $125 per visit — that's $35 off the full detail price. You get priority booking, locked-in pricing, and 10% off any add-ons. Limited to 5 spots total. Email me to join.

How much does mobile car detailing cost in Snohomish, WA?

Interior detail starts at $160, exterior starts at $130, and a full detail (interior + exterior) starts at $260. Pricing scales with vehicle size. Use the quote calculator above for an instant estimate.

Do you guarantee your work?

100%. If you're not happy with any part of the service, I'll fix it on the spot, come back free, or refund you. I don't leave until you love it.

What areas in Snohomish County do you serve?

I serve Snohomish, Monroe, Lake Stevens, Everett, Bothell, Mill Creek, Duvall, and surrounding areas. Not sure if I cover your area? Give me a call.
