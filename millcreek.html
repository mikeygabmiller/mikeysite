Common Questions

Mill Creek FAQ

Stuff drivers ask before booking.

Do you come to Mill Creek?

Yes. I service all of Mill Creek — Town Center, the Country Club neighborhoods, North Creek, the 132nd and 164th corridors, Silver Firs, and the Bothell-Everett Highway side streets.

Will my HOA have a problem?

Most HOAs in Mill Creek are fine with mobile detailing — it's a passenger-vehicle setup, not a commercial truck or noisy generator. If your HOA has specific rules, mention them when booking.

How much does it cost?

Interior starts at $160. Exterior starts at $130. Full detail starts at $260. Use the instant quote tool for an exact price.

How long does a full detail take?

First-time full details run 3–4 hours. Clean Club members get maintenance details in about an hour.

Do I need to be home?

No. Most Mill Creek clients leave the keys, head to Town Center, and come back to a finished car.

What if it rains?

I have a pop-up canopy that covers most cars under 6 feet tall. For heavy weather, we can reschedule.
