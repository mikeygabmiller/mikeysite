The Process

Step 1, Decontamination Wash

Two-bucket hand wash, iron remover to dissolve embedded brake dust, clay bar to pull out anything still stuck. Paint has to be perfectly clean before correction.

Step 2, Paint Correction

This is where I remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation. Coating locks in whatever is on the paint, so this step matters. Skip it and you're sealing in flaws for 5 years.

Step 3, Surface Prep

Panel wipe-down with isopropyl to strip any residual oils or polishing compounds. The coating needs bare, clean clear coat to bond.

Step 4, Coating Application

Applied panel by panel with applicator and microfiber. Cures over 24 hours. Vehicle needs to stay dry during the cure window, garage or covered space required overnight.

Step 5, Aftercare Plan

I walk you through how to maintain it. Hand wash with pH-neutral soap, no automatic brush washes, top up with maintenance spray every 6 months. Coating lasts way longer when it's cared for right.

Honest Take Ceramic coating isn't a magic shield. Rocks still chip, brushes still scratch. What it does is protect against chemical and UV damage, make washing way easier, and keep your paint looking new for years instead of months. Common Questions

How long does the application take?

4-8 hours for the coating itself. With paint correction included, plan a full day. Vehicle stays put for 24 hours after to cure.

Do I need to be home?

You need to be home at the start to confirm the work and at the end to walk through aftercare. In between, you're free.

What if it rains?

Application happens in your garage or under coverage. The 24-hour cure window also needs dry shelter. We'll plan around weather when we book.

Can you coat a brand-new car?

Yes, and it's the ideal time. Brand-new paint with no swirl marks gets the cleanest coating result. Many customers schedule with their dealership delivery date.

What about wheels and glass?

Wheel face coating and glass coating are included starting at the Standard tier. Brake dust wipes off coated wheels way easier, and rain sheets off coated windshields without needing wipers as much.
