What Affects The Timing

1. Size of your vehicle

A Civic and a Suburban are not the same job. A compact sedan might shave 30-45 minutes off a full detail. A three-row SUV adds an hour easy. Trucks with bed liners and crew cabs land somewhere in the middle.

2. Condition

If your car gets daily use with kids, pets, or job site dirt, it takes longer than a weekend cruiser. A car that hasn't been detailed in 2 years versus one that was done 3 months ago - totally different jobs even if you book the same package.

3. Add-ons

Pet hair, headlight restoration, engine bay cleaning, and odor removal all add real time. I'll tell you upfront on the quote so you're not waiting around.

Real Example A regular Clean Club member's sedan? About 2 hours. Same package on a first-time customer's neglected truck with dog hair? Closer to 4. Same price tier, very different job.

How To Plan Your Day

  • Basic interior or exterior? Book it during your lunch break. Done before you need the car back.
  • Full detail? Book the morning. Car's ready by mid-afternoon.
  • Paint correction or ceramic coating? Plan a full day. Sometimes overnight if you're doing the coating cure.
