    Common Questions

    Everything You're Wondering

    Do you really come to me for an oil change?

    Yep, that's the whole idea. I pull up to your home or work in Snohomish County with the oil, filters, tools and a catch pan. You hand me the keys and get on with your day while I service your car right in the driveway.

    What kind of maintenance do you do?

    Light auto maintenance: oil and filter changes, fluid top-offs and flushes (coolant, brake, transmission, washer), air and cabin filters, plus battery, wiper blade and bulb replacement. Think Jiffy Lube or Grease Monkey service, but I come to you. For anything bigger I'll point you to a trusted shop.

    How long does a mobile oil change take?

    Most oil and filter changes take about 30 to 45 minutes. Fluids, filters and quick swaps like wipers, bulbs or a battery are usually under 30 minutes. I'll give you a clear time window when we confirm.

    What does it cost?

    Oil and filter changes start at $89, fluids and filters at $59, and quick swaps like wipers and bulbs start at $25. Run the Quick Quote above for a price in about 30 seconds, no surprises, no hidden fees.

    When and how do I pay?

    You pay when the work is done and you're happy with it, not before. I take cash, card, and the usual apps.

    What do you do with the used oil?

    I haul away your old oil and filter and recycle them properly at a certified facility. You don't have to deal with the mess or the disposal.

    What if something's not right after the service?

    We look it over together before I leave. See something off? I fix it on the spot. Notice it later? I come back and make it right, no charge. You don't pay until the job's done right.

    Still have a question? Just ask me.
