The Challenge

Why Standard Vacuums Fail

If you've ever tried to vacuum out Golden Retriever or Husky hair from your car's carpeting, you know the frustration. Pet hair doesn't just sit on top of the fabric—it acts like tiny needles, weaving itself deep into the carpet fibers of your floors and seats.

A gas station vacuum simply doesn't have the suction or the mechanical action required to pull those hairs out. It requires specialized detailing tools. I use pumice stones, rubber lily brushes, compressed air, and high-powered extraction vacuums to agitate the fibers and pull the hair free.

Whether you are returning a lease, selling your car, or just want to drive without getting covered in fur, our pet hair removal process brings your interior back to a clean, fresh state.

How It Works

The Pet Hair Extraction Process

A specialized approach to lifting and removing stubborn fur.

Dry Brushing & Agitation

We use specialized rubber brushes and pumice stones to scrape the carpets, pulling woven-in hairs to the surface without damaging the fabric.

High-Powered Vacuuming

Using a strong shop-vac combined with compressed air, we blow out the deep crevices and immediately vacuum up the loosened fur.

Shampoo & Steam (Optional)

For vehicles with "wet dog" smells, we follow up with steam cleaning and upholstery shampooing to break down dander and odors.

Final Inspection

We do a final pass over the seats, floor mats, and cargo areas to ensure your car looks and smells clean again.

Common Questions

Pet Hair FAQ

Everything you need to know about our hair removal service.

Can you get every single pet hair out?

While we strive for perfection and use specialized tools, some incredibly short, stiff hairs weave themselves deeply into certain types of carpet. We typically achieve 95-99% removal depending on the fabric.

Does pet hair removal cost extra?

Yes, severe pet hair requires significant extra time and manual labor beyond a standard interior vacuum. It is usually treated as an add-on to our interior detailing packages, depending on severity.

Will this remove the "wet dog" smell?

Removing the hair and dander is the first step. By combining hair removal with deep steam cleaning and shampooing of the upholstery, we can neutralize and eliminate lingering pet odors.

Do you clean the seats as well as the floors?

Absolutely. Pet hair gets everywhere—cloth seats, floor mats, cargo areas, and even the headliner. Our process covers the entire interior cabin.
