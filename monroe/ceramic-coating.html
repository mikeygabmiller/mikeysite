Common Questions

Monroe Ceramic FAQ

Stuff drivers ask before booking a coating.

Do you do ceramic coating in Monroe, WA?

Yes. Monroe is one of my core service cities. I cover Downtown, Fryelands Blvd, Sky Valley, and the surrounding 98272 area. No travel surcharge.

How much does ceramic coating cost?

2-Year Starter from $600. 5-Year Pro from $1,100. 7-Year Elite from $1,800. Final price depends on vehicle size and condition, use the instant quote tool for an exact number.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Entry coatings last 2 years. Mid-tier runs 3-5 years. Pro-grade 9H coatings last 7+ years with proper maintenance washes.

How long does the job take?

6-10 hours including paint decontamination and prep. The car needs to stay dry for 24 hours after, so book it for a day you don't need to drive.

Ceramic coating vs wax, what's the difference?

Wax lasts 2-3 months. Ceramic lasts 2-7 years and chemically bonds to your paint. If you wax twice a year, one coating pays for itself in 3 years.
