Common Questions

Snohomish Truck FAQ

Stuff drivers ask before booking a detail.

Do you detail trucks in Snohomish, WA?

Yes. Snohomish is my primary service city. I cover Historic Downtown, Pilchuck, Three Lakes, Bickford, and the surrounding 98290 area. No travel surcharge.

How much does truck detailing cost?

An Exterior Detail starts at $200. An Interior Detail runs $240+. A Full Detail is $340+. Final price depends on the size of the rig and how much mud or pet hair we are dealing with, use the instant quote tool for an exact number.

How long will the results last?

A full detail includes a spray sealant that protects the paint for up to 6 months. Interior freshness depends on how heavily the truck is used, but deep extraction resets the fabric to day one.

How long does the job take?

A standard truck detail takes 3-5 hours. Heavy mud or deep stain extraction can add some time. I do the entire job right where you park.

Do you detail lifted trucks or heavily muddied rigs?

Absolutely. I bring step ladders for lifted roofs and heavy-duty pressure washing equipment to blow out caked-on mud from the undercarriage and wheel wells.
