Common Questions.

How much does exterior car detailing cost in Snohomish?
Exterior detailing in Snohomish runs $160-$240 depending on vehicle size. Sedans land near the low end, full-size trucks near the top. Use the quote calculator on the homepage for an exact price, or see the full pricing breakdown.
How long does an exterior detail take?
Most exterior details take 1.5-2.5 hours. Heavy mud, sap, or oxidation can push it longer. See the full time breakdown.
Do you need access to water and power?
Yes. I'll need access to a water spigot and a power outlet. Beyond that, just a spot to park, driveway, parking lot, or street works fine.
Will the sealant protect my paint long term?
The included spray sealant gives roughly 2-3 months of protection, water beading, easier cleaning, light UV defense. For multi-year protection, look at ceramic coating.
What if it rains on my appointment day?
Light rain isn't an issue, I work under cover or reschedule on the spot at no charge. Heavy rain or a downpour, I'll reach out the day before and we'll find a better time.
Do I need to be home during the service?
No. As long as I have access to the car and a place to park, you can be at work or running errands. I'll text when I'm done.
What areas around Snohomish do you cover?
I'm based in Snohomish and serve Monroe, Lake Stevens, Everett, Bothell, Mill Creek, Duvall, and surrounding Snohomish County. Not sure if I cover yours? Give me a call.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
We do a walkthrough together when I'm done. Anything off gets fixed on the spot. Notice something later? I come back free. Still not 100%? Full refund. I don't leave until you love it.
