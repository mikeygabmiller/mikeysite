Common Questions

Near Me FAQ

Stuff people ask before booking.

What is mobile car detailing?

The detailer brings the full setup, water, power, polishers, vacuums, products, to your home or workplace. The car never leaves your driveway. Same work as a shop, your location.

How much does it cost near me?

In Snohomish County: interior starts at $200, exterior $160, full detail $299. Trucks and heavily-soiled vehicles run higher. The instant quote tool gives an exact number in 30 seconds.

How close do I need to be?

I cover all of Snohomish County: Snohomish, Monroe, Lake Stevens, Everett, Marysville, Mukilteo, Mill Creek, Lynnwood, Edmonds, Granite Falls, Duvall, Carnation, and surrounding areas. No travel surcharge inside the county.

Do I need to be home?

No. Leave the keys, head to work, come back to a clean car. Most clients pay through a text invoice link after I send before-and-after photos.

What do you need from me to detail my car?

Access to a water spigot and a power outlet to run my equipment, plus a spot to park. If you're not sure what's available at your place, just mention it when you book and we'll figure out the closest hookup.
